Game Development

A game developer is someone who works on video games. They are also known as a software developer. Here is an example of something that a game developer made.

Game development is very difficult because depending on the team size you might have to do art, coding, music, and so on.

GDD

A GDD (Game Design Document) is the first step in making a game. The GDD is a document that goes over the basic idea of the game so everyone on the team is on the same page.

A GDD will iclude these normally.

  • Concept Art
  • Game Mechanics
  • Story
  • Level/environment design
  • Characters
  • Sound and Music
  • User Interface, Game Controls

Game Engine

A Game Engine is a software that is used to create the game. You can choose from a selection of engines or you can make your own.

Making your own is very difficult so I recomend that you use something for example Unity, Unreal Engine, or Game Maker Studio.
